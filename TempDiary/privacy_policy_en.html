TempDiary Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2025

TempDiary is a health utility app developed by FreeForm Software Studio for recording body temperature and medication usage. We highly value your privacy and personal data protection. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information.

1. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

When using TempDiary, we may collect the following types of information:

(1) Information You Manually Enter

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server:

  • Body temperature records, medication logs
  • Profile information (e.g., nickname, gender, date of birth, avatar)
  • Notes and other health-related information

This data is only used to support core features such as health logging, data visualization, and PDF export.

(2) Information Automatically Collected by Third-Party SDKs

We use third-party services for advertising, crash analysis, and performance optimization. These services may collect the following information:

Data Type Purpose Provider
Device Identifiers (e.g., IDFA, Instance ID) Ad delivery and attribution Google AdMob
App Usage (e.g., page views, feature usage) Usage analytics Firebase Analytics
Crash logs and errors Improve stability Firebase Crashlytics
Performance data (e.g., launch time, responsiveness) Optimize experience Firebase Performance

If you are in the EU, you may disable personalized ads within the app settings to limit tracking.

2. How We Store and Protect Your Personal Information

  • All health records and profile data are stored locally on your device. We do not access or store any plaintext data.
  • No data is uploaded to a server or synced with Apple Health or iCloud.
  • All data is saved in a secure local database within the iOS system sandbox, inaccessible to other apps or external parties without your consent.
  • We do not upload or sync any health data in the background.
  • Third-party SDKs only collect essential analytics data and follow their own privacy policies.

3. How We Share, Transfer, or Disclose Your Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with any companies, organizations, or individuals, except in the following cases:

  1. Automatically shared anonymized device data with third-party service providers (e.g., Google) for advertising, crash analysis, and performance tracking
  2. As required by applicable laws or government authorities
  3. With your explicit consent or authorization

We do not sell, trade, transfer, or publicly disclose your personal information for commercial purposes.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or TempDiary, you can contact us:

  • Email: support@freeformstudio.cn
  • Developer Name: FreeForm Software Studio

We will respond within 15 business days of receiving your inquiry.

If we make any updates to this policy, we will notify you via the App Store page and within the app. Thank you for trusting TempDiary.
